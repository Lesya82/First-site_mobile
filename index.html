About me

Hi, I'm Lesya, one of the top Lviv photographers and videographers. I make beautiful, emotive photographs that you'll love and be proud of. I'm friendly and personable and once you've enquired I like to set about getting to know you and your requirements - the first all-important step in working with you to capture fabulous images. I'm based in Lviv, Ukraine . I love getting creative with you and our amazing sunny locations, all essential ingredients that come together to make the magic happen. I work in my own studio and at the clients' facilities. I specialise in all sorts of children photography striving to deliver timeless, natural light portraits that you will treasure forever.

Studio

On the territory of the studio there are 7 decorated backgrounds, suitable for professional and conceptual photography with different subjects. Young visitors and their adult companions are offered the help of experienced makeup artists and stylists who will pick up a unique image and emphasize the individuality of the baby of any age. A large selection of props is sure to interest children, and professional lighting equipment and photography help to capture them during the game, so all the pictures are perfect. Our creative photo studio offers not just to take photos, but to create a mood and create emotions, turning them into memory. To meet the expectations of parents, we have carefully prepared and arranged children's photo areas, with a cozy interior, where each child reveals the talent of a model. Children's photo shoot is an opportunity to get quality and beautiful, themed and creative photos of children who will decorate the house and children's room, will occupy worthy place in your family photo album.
